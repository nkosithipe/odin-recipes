Nut Spinach Recipe

Though we all know very well the health benefits of Spinach, however we still find kids, teenagers and young adults leaving it on their plates. At times even some adults shun away from this natural energizer side dish packed with potassium as well as tons of detoxifying agents.

The dish may be enjoyed alongside with some starch such as pap or roasted potatoes. Many peri-urban and rural South African homes, particularly the middle-class and lower income house-holds often delight on Spinach as a relish.

Seeing that the vegetable is not luxurious but valuable just like water. The Nut Spinach recipe adds that kind of value which compels royalty to visit a peasant's house. From the aroma to the dance of the bolus on your tongue. This recipe will turn-on any hater of Spinach, try it for yourself!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tsp Oil
  • 1 Small Onion
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1,2kg of Spinach
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1/4 cup powdered peanuts

Method

  1. Peel and boil potatoes with plain water for 15 minutes
  2. Remove potatoes from heat and spill out the water
  3. Heat oil in a different pot and add seasonings with the onion finely diced.
  4. Saute for few minutes until onions are cooked
  5. Add the earlier prepared potatoes
  6. Immediately add the spinach and salt then allow to cook for 12 minutes
  7. When spinach is cooked, sprinkle the peanut powder and mix well mashing everything in the pot
  8. Bring to low heat and allow simmering for 10 minutes
